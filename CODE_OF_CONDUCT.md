# Code of Conduct

## Introduction

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone participating in our project. Our Code of Conduct outlines the principles and guidelines that we expect all contributors to adhere to. This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions, including discussions on our forums, in issue trackers, and during events.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of unacceptable behavior include, but are not limited to:

- **Harassment**: Using offensive comments, jokes, or images; engaging in personal attacks or bullying; or any other behavior that is hostile or intimidating.
- **Discrimination**: Excluding, intimidating, or discriminating against individuals based on their personal attributes or experiences.
- **Unwelcome Attention**: Repeatedly contacting someone after they have indicated they are not interested, or using aggressive or inappropriate language.
- **Threats**: Making threats of any kind, including threats of violence or retaliation.

## Responsibilities

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all contributors with respect, valuing their input and opinions, even if you disagree with them.
- **Communicate Constructively**: Provide constructive feedback and suggestions in a manner that is respectful and supportive.
- **Report Issues**: If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers. We will take all reports seriously and investigate them promptly.

## Enforcement

If you violate this Code of Conduct, the project maintainers may take appropriate action, which could include:

- **Warning**: Issuing a warning to the individual, requesting them to cease the unacceptable behavior.
- **Temporary Ban**: Temporarily suspending the individual's participation in the project.
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanently removing the individual from the project.

The severity of the action will depend on the nature of the violation and the circumstances surrounding it.
